    • Kazi Nazrul Islam

    The national poet of Bangladesh

    Rebel Poet

    Kazi Nazrul Islam is the national poet of Bangladesh who is especially recognized as a Birodhi Kobi (rebel poet) around the country.Nazrul is a fameous poet, musician and philosopher of Bangladesh. The fameous poet Nazrul's writing deal with the revolution of that period of the british government. His revolutionary attempt helped him to be a national poet of Bangladesh. He has raised his voice against any kind of fasicsm and oppression and got popularity swiftly around the Indian Subcontinent.

    Awesome Writer

    Nazrul's writings explored themes such as lovve, freedom, humanity and revolution. He opposed all forms of bigotry and fundamentalism, including religious, case-based and gender-based. Throughtout his career, Nazrul wrote short stories, novels, and essay but is best known for his songs and poems. He pioneered new music forms such as Bengali ghazals. Nazrul wrote and composed music for nearly 4,000 songs many rocorded on HMV and gramophone records), collectively known as Nazrul Geeti
